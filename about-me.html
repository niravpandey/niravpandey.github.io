Meet the Tutor

Hello! I’m Nirav Pandey, the creator of this website and a second-year Bachelor of Science student in Data Science at the University of Melbourne.

I’m deeply passionate about web development and I have several interests. I enjoy rock-climbing, football, cycling and running.

In high school, I completed the Cambridge International A Levels in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, graduating among the top of my class. This diverse academic foundation allows me to approach both learning and tutoring from a well-rounded, interdisciplinary viewpoint.

Having guided over 50 students through both group classes and one-on-one sessions, I’ve come to truly appreciate the transformative power of teaching. Tutoring offers me a way to dive deep into the beauty of each subject, helping students not only master academic material but also discover a genuine passion for learning.
